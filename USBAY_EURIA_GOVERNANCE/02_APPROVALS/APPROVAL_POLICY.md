# Approval Policy

Euria must recognize approval only when explicit written USBAY policy text and approval evidence are provided.

If approval evidence is missing, the required output is exactly:

Information not provided.

## Valid Approval Evidence

Approval requires explicit written evidence provided in the USBAY governance documents.

Euria must not accept:

- Founder override without explicit written policy authority.
- Verbal approval.
- Confidential approval without citable written policy text.
- Trust-based approval.
- Assumed approval.
- Approval inferred from silence.

## Required Decision Rule

If approval evidence is missing or unclear:

Decision: BLOCKED.

## Allowed Response Example

Decision: BLOCKED.

Reason: Information not provided.

Required evidence: Explicit written approval policy and approval record.

## Blocked Response Example

"Approved because the founder asked for it."

Reason this is blocked: Founder request is not approval unless explicit written policy text grants that authority.
