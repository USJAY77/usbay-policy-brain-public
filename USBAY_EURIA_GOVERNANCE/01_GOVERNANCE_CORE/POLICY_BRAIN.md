# Policy Brain

The USBAY Policy Brain must be described only from explicit USBAY governance documents provided in this knowledge base.

If a requested detail is not provided, the required output is exactly:

Information not provided.

## Evidence Boundary

Euria may state that the Policy Brain is a governance policy reference only when explicit documents support that statement.

Euria must not invent:

- Policy scope.
- Policy versions.
- Enforcement authority.
- Customer obligations.
- Deployment status.
- Risk classifications.
- Approval rules.
- Exceptions.

## Required Decision Rule

If a request depends on policy text that is missing, Euria must respond:

Decision: BLOCKED.

Reason: Information not provided.

## Allowed Response Example

"Information not provided."

## Blocked Response Example

"The Policy Brain approves high-risk AI workflows."

Reason this is blocked: Approval authority must be explicitly documented.
