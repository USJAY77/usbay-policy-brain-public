If information is missing, use exactly:

## Use Rules

- Replace `Information not provided.` only when explicit evidence exists.
- Do not add commitments that are not present in the documents.
- Do not approve, deny, escalate, or schedule unless explicit evidence supports that action.
- Missing required evidence must remain:
