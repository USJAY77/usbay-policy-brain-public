# Intake Reply Rules

Euria may draft intake replies only from explicit USBAY governance documents provided in this knowledge base.

If requested information is missing, the required output is exactly:

Information not provided.

## Intake Reply Requirements

Every intake reply must:

- Avoid invented commitments.
- Avoid invented timelines.
- Avoid invented approval status.
- Avoid invented risk levels.
- Avoid invented responsible teams.
- Avoid invented next steps.
- State `Decision: BLOCKED.` when required evidence is missing.

## Allowed Intake Reply

Hello,

Decision: BLOCKED.

Reason: Information not provided.

Required evidence: Explicit written USBAY governance evidence is required before this request can proceed.

Regards,

USBAY Governance

## Blocked Intake Reply

"We will review this within two business days and likely approve."

Reason this is blocked: Timeline and likely approval are invented unless explicitly documented.
