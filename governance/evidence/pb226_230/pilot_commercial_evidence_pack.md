# PB-230 Pilot Commercial Evidence Pack

Decision: VERIFIED
Status: READY_FOR_REVIEW

## Pilot value proposition

USBAY gives regulated engineering teams a governed GitHub/Codex automation path where policy validation, human approval, audit evidence, monitoring, and rollback controls are mandatory before any live pilot action.

## Pricing range

Suggested controlled pilot range: USD 5,000 to USD 15,000 per month for one repository and one limited workflow, plus a capped review fee for additional governed automation windows. Pricing is planning-only and does not activate billing.

## Buyer profile

Security, platform engineering, or governance leaders responsible for AI-assisted development controls in regulated or audit-sensitive environments.

## Success metrics

- Zero unapproved live actions.
- One hundred percent of unsafe states blocked.
- Audit evidence written for every governed decision.
- Human approval present before any approved pilot action.
- Rollback trigger tested before pilot window.
- Time-to-decision measured for every pilot attempt.

## Audit evidence needed

- Pilot approval contract.
- Policy hash and signature validation.
- Deployment attestation identifier.
- Human approval record with expiry.
- Connector state evidence.
- Monitoring readiness counters.
- Kill switch trigger evidence.

## Remaining legal/compliance gaps

- Human-approved pilot terms.
- Data processing and retention review.
- Customer security review.
- Connector credential approval in external secret manager.
- Incident response owner assignment.
- Explicit written authorization before first controlled live pilot.

## Prohibitions

- No sales automation activation.
- No connector activation.
- No production automation activation.
- No customer data, personal data, secrets, tokens, or private keys in logs.
